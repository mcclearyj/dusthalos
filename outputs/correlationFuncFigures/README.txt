This directory contains plots of the galaxy/dust correlation functions for a variety of redMaGiC galaxy 
(a/k/a the background sample) redshift bins. 


av_wiseScosSvm.png shows the intrinsic spread in the Av of the foreground galaxies; the "variance" is actually
the weight from the MLE. 

dustCorr_orthonorm-v*-rm.png is the result of correlating HIZ redmagic sample (0.55<z<0.90) as fg with LOWZ redmagic sample (0.15<z<0.45) as background.

dustCorr_orthonorm-v*-rm2.png is the result of correlating the SCOS sample (as a foreground) with the just the HIZ redmagic sample as a background. Hi-z is 0.55<z<0.90. 

dustCorr_orthonorm-v*-rm3.is the result of correlating the FULL redmagic sample as a background with the HIZ redMaGiC sample as a foreground. 
 - related: redMaGiC-Scos_nullTest3.png shows this same correlation with a more appropriate axis range
 
dustCorr_orthonorm-v*-rm4.is the result of correlating the FULL redmagic sample as a background with the MIDZ redMaGiC sample as a foreground. Mid-z is 0.15<z<0.45
