This directory contains plots of the galaxy/dust correlation functions for a variety of redMaGiC galaxy 
(a/k/a the background sample) redshift bins. 

dust_corr.png shows the default correlation function for nbins = 10

The effect of different random subtractions are shown in dust_signal_variousRandom_subtractions.png

av_wiseScosSvm.png shows the intrinsic spread in the Av of the foreground galaxies; the "variance" is actually
the weight from the MLE.
